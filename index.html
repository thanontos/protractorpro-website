Features
Everything you need to bid with confidence
ProtractorPro is built around the way contractors actually work — from plans to pricebook to signed contract.
📐

Plan Digitizer

Load any PDF plan and draw measurements directly on-screen. Polygon, rectangle, linear, and count tools let you capture every detail of a job.

🏷

Smart Conditions

Define named areas like "Main Field," "Ridge Cap," or "Valley." Each condition auto-generates the right quantities and pulls from your material layers.

📦

Material Library

Maintain a searchable library of your materials, labor rates, and assemblies. Import line items into any bid with a single click.

💰

Accurate Pricing

Apply overhead, profit margin, and tax with precision. Know your real cost vs. sell price before you send the bid.

📏

Pitch & Scale

Set your roof pitch factor and drawing scale once per page. All measurements auto-correct so your square counts are always right.

🔒

Multi-User Locking

Working on the same bid as a colleague? Real-time lock indicators prevent overwrite conflicts and keep your team in sync.
